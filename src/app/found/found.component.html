How to Do Good and Get Paid!

We are so glad you scanned this lost item. To get paid for helping the owner, follow these quick instructions

  1. Take the Lost Item and head to the nearest UPS store
  2. Enter Approximate Weight of the Package in ounces or pounds (label with the unit)
  3. Give the package to a UPS worker so that they can scan the shipping label
  4. Once scanned, the owner will be notified and ItMine.co will gift you money
  5. Enter Your Email and we will send you your reward once the package arrives at the owner's home
